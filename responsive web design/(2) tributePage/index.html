Seneca

(Lucius Annaeus Seneca the Younger)

4 BC – 65 AD (68-69 years old)

Seneca facts

  • He was born Córboda in Hispania but raised in Rome.
  • His father was Seneca the Elder who was a wealthy roman writer.
  • Seneca's brother (Lucius) was a roman senator
  • Seneca took his life due to alligations of complicity in the assassination of Emperor Nero which he was probably innocent of
  • Seneca is famous for his calm and stoic suicide
